How to Play
  1. Each day brings 5 classic rock songs from the golden era (1960–1980).
  2. Click the vinyl record to play a real audio clip. Starts at just 1 second.
  3. Type your guess — song title only. Partial title works: "Stairway" counts.
  4. Skip to hear a longer clip, but you lose points each time.
  5. Scoring: Attempt 1=100, 2=80, 3=60, 4=40, 5=20, Miss=0 pts.
  6. After each round, learn the story behind the song.
  7. Max 500 points. Everyone gets the same 5 songs. New puzzle at midnight.
